Multiple Sequence Free-Alignment (MSFA)

Alignment-free methods are based on word-counting, which only con- siders the frequency of joint neighboring words without any correction of their locations in the sequence and it is of interest to track the position of nucleotides in sequence and use the underlying distribution of data. MSFSA is generally the alignment-free of three or more biological sequences (protein or nucleic acid) of similar\different length. The result is a dendrogram that can be used to draw the inference and diagnosis the evolutionary relationships between the sequences studied.
